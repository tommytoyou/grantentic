Frequently Asked Questions

What do I get with the one-time purchase?

You get access to generate up to 3 Pre-Pitches and 1 complete SBIR Phase I proposal for any of our supported agencies (NSF, DoD, NASA). This includes unlimited revisions for 30 days.

What is a Pre-Pitch?

A Pre-Pitch is a shorter preliminary document (like NSF's Project Pitch) that agencies use to screen applicants before inviting them to submit a full Phase I proposal. It's typically 1-2 pages and helps you get early feedback on your idea.

How long does it take to generate a proposal?

A Pre-Pitch typically generates in 2-3 minutes. A complete Phase I proposal takes 5-10 minutes, depending on the agency and the complexity of your company's information.

Can I edit the generated proposal?

Yes! The proposal is exported as a Word document that you can fully edit and customize before submission.

What agencies are supported?

We currently support NSF, DoD, and NASA SBIR Phase I proposals. Each agency has specific requirements that our AI is trained to follow.
